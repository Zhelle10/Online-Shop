About Us

Wychwood Art Gallery sells contemporary art online, in their Cotswold art gallery which is based in Deddington and at the Affordable Art Fair in Battersea, London.
Representing over 400 contemporary artists and offering a free art advisory
service.

Our chief art curator, Deborah Allan, after studying History of Art and Fine Art at university worked at Christie’s auctioneers for 11 years as Director of Impressionist and Modern Art. Deborah was then head hunted by Bonhams where she was
European Director for 4 years before she set up Wychwood Art gallery.

Wychwood Art Gallery represent some of the U.K’s most exceptional contemporary artists. Listed here are some of our artists that we represent, showing you our
diverse and careful selection of original art and limited edition prints.

FAST RELIABLE DELIVERY

We offer free, secure and insured UK delivery. For international deliveries there is a rate of £50 which is also sent securely and insured. Once a work is dispatched, tracking is sent with an estimated delivery date. Works are guaranteed to be with you within 5-7 days, unless we email or call you to let you know that it will be longer. Next day delivery is available at a small cost. Please contact us to discuss a next day delivery. USA deliveries take 2 days, unless we call you to let you know that it will be longer. EXPERTLY CURATED COLLECTION

The artworks are all hand selected by the Director of Wychwood Art, Deborah Allan. Deborah has worked in the international art world for over 15 years, firstly at Christie’s for 11 years as Director of Impressionist, Modern Art, and subsequently at Bonhams for 4 years as European Director of Impressionist and Modern Art giving her invaluable knowledge of the art market. Selling art as an auctioneer to important art collectors has given her great insight into why people buy art and what makes a good work of art. You can be confident that whatever work of art you choose from our collection, it will be of high quality and good value. Deborah is also well placed to advise on art advisory. If you have any questions regarding buying art as an investment, please contact us.
